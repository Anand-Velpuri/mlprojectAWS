What goes into the prediction?

The model weighs multiple factors shown to correlate with academic outcomes.

Demographics

Gender and race/ethnicity group are used as contextual signals in the model.

Parental education

A parent's highest qualification — from high school to master's degree — is a strong predictor.

Lunch type

Standard vs free/reduced lunch is a well-studied proxy for socioeconomic background.

Test preparation

Whether the student completed a preparation course before the exam.

Reading score

Strong readers tend to perform better across all subjects, including mathematics.

Writing score

Writing proficiency correlates with reasoning skills that underpin math performance.
